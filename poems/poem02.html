random access[1]

The title is taken from the description of Nam June Paik's work 〈Random Access Audio Tape〉. Random access refers to the capability to access every item in a list within a specified time. (Source: Nam June Paik Art Center official Twitter account. Last modified June 19, 2025. Last accessed July 5, 2025. URL: https://x.com/NamJunePaikArt/status/1935593467302047980)
